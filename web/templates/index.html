What This Project Demonstrates

VLSI / Standard Cells

Characterizes INV, NAND2 and DFF cells across Process-Voltage-Temperature corners — the timing data foundries ship to chip designers as Liberty (.lib) files.

Analog / SPICE

A parametric ngspice testbench (with a physics-grounded analytic fallback) sweeps PVT corners, drive strength and load to build a ~12k-point delay dataset.

ML for EDA

A PyTorch MLP learns the underlying delay physics and predicts in microseconds — a surrogate that regenerates timing tables thousands of times faster than SPICE.
